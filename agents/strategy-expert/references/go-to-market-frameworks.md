### 2. Channel Strategy

| Channel | Best for | CAC | Time to Revenue |
|---------|---------|-----|----------------|
| Direct Sales | Enterprise, high ACV | Cao | Dài (3-12 tháng) |
| Inside Sales | SMB, mid-market | Trung bình | Trung bình |
| Self-serve | PLG, low ACV | Thấp | Nhanh |
| Partner/Reseller | Scale, market coverage | Thấp-trung | Tùy partner |
| Content/SEO | Long-term, organic | Rất thấp | Chậm |

**Khuyến nghị theo ACV (Annual Contract Value):**
- ACV < $1,000: Self-serve / PLG
- ACV $1,000-$10,000: Inside Sales + Self-serve
- ACV > $10,000: Direct Sales + Partner

### 3. Pricing Strategy

| Strategy | Mô tả | Dùng khi |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Per-seat | Tính theo số user | SaaS B2B, usage predictable |
| Per-usage | Tính theo lượng dùng | Volume uncertain |
| Flat fee | Giá cố định | Simple, easy to explain |
| Freemium | Free + paid upgrade | Virality, high volume |
| Value-based | Tính theo value delivered | Enterprise, clear ROI |

**VN Market specific:**
- SMB nhạy cảm với giá → Freemium hoặc low entry price
- Enterprise VN: procurement process dài, cần PO approval
- Trial period: 14-30 ngày được kỳ vọng cho SaaS

### 5. GTM Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target (SaaS B2B VN) |
|--------|---------|---------------------|
| CAC | Marketing + Sales Cost / New Customers | < 12 months LTV |
| LTV | ARPU × Avg Customer Lifespan | LTV:CAC > 3:1 |
| MRR Growth | (MRR end - MRR start) / MRR start | > 15%/tháng early stage |
| Churn Rate | Churned MRR / Starting MRR | < 3%/tháng |
| NRR | (Start MRR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Start MRR | > 100% |
