## Cash Flow Timing

### Typical B2B SaaS Cash Flow

```
Month 1-3: Negative (development, setup)
Month 4-6: Near-zero (early customers, high CAC)
Month 7-12: Approaching breakeven (MRR building)
Month 13-18: Positive (scale kicks in)
Month 24+: Profitable (if unit economics healthy)
```

### Tối ưu Cash Flow

- Ưu tiên **annual contracts** (12× upfront) vs monthly
- **Deposits** cho custom development (30-50% upfront)
- **Net 30-45** payment terms, không Net 90+
- **Early payment discount**: 2% nếu thanh toán trong 10 ngày

| Metric | Minimum viable | Target |

## Funding & Capital Requirements

### Bootstrap vs Funded

**Bootstrap viable nếu:**
- Total investment < 2 tỷ VND
- Founder có technical chops → giảm dev cost
- Revenue từ tháng 6-12 có thể tự nuôi

**Cần external funding nếu:**
- Investment > 5 tỷ VND
- Time to market critical (race condition)
- Sales team cần trước khi có revenue
